Vijay


Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, also known as Vijay An actor, dancer, playback singer, and philanthropist in Tamil cinema. He was born in Chennai on June 22, 1974. His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a film director and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer. Vijay is known for his acting skills and dialogue delivery. He made his debut in the 1992 film Naalaya Theerpu and his career took off with the 2004 action film Ghilli. He has won several awards, including eight Vijay Awards and three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. He is also known as "Thalapathy" (commander).
